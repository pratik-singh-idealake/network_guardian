## 0.0.1

* Initial release
* Real internet verification via HTTP check — not just WiFi status
* Auto toast notification slides in when internet is lost
* Auto hides toast when internet is restored
* Customizable toast widget support — bring your own UI
* Toast position support — top or bottom of screen
* Configurable toast duration
* Battery optimized — pauses monitoring when app goes to background
* Resumes monitoring when app comes to foreground
* Android and iOS support
